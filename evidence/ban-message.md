# Account Ban Notice

After 24 hours of legitimate operation with 200,000+ real impressions, the following message appeared:

---

> **Your account is permanently blocked**
>
> This account has been suspended for violating our Terms and Conditions.
> We can no longer provide our services to you. All funds in your account will be refunded to advertisers.

---

## Context

- **200,000+ impressions** from real human traffic in the first 24 hours
- Zero bot traffic, zero self-clicks, zero traffic manipulation
- Revenue accrued to ~$35
- All revenue was seized and "refunded to advertisers"
- No specific explanation of which clause was violated
- No evidence provided
- No appeal process offered

## What actually happened

The publisher attempted to protect users from Adsterra's redirect creatives (page hijacking) by using standard browser security features (iframe sandbox attributes). Adsterra's "LieDetector" system detected this protection and permanently banned the account.

**In other words:** Adsterra bans publishers who try to protect their users from Adsterra's own malicious ad behavior.

## The money

- Advertisers paid for 200,000+ impressions that were actually delivered to real users
- Adsterra collected from advertisers
- Publisher received $0
- Adsterra claims to have "refunded" the money to advertisers
- Whether that refund actually happens is unverifiable
